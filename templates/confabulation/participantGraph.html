What is this?
This page shows the graph generated from all chains, themes and stories of {{name}}. The image is zoomable, and the individual nodes can be dragged around with your mouse.
Legend:
  • Big circles represent chains
  • Triangles represent themes
  • Dots represent stories
  • Yellow edges and backgrounds indicate inter-connections


The page programmatically generates the graph. The arrangement of the nodes is calculated by a physical model that includes springs, gravitational forces and repulsion. It is a dynamic representation, if the position of a node changes, the other nodes rearrange themselves around it.

Please be aware that this representation is computation-heavy; consequently, it calculates on the client side, in your browser. Therefore it can make your computer slow or start the ventilation. If this happens, just navigate away.
